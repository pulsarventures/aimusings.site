## About

Sree Pradhip is a builder and evangelist helping startup founders and technical leaders design and operate AI-first systems. He works at the intersection of engineering, product, strategy, and execution — building real systems while also helping teams understand the structural shift AI represents.

As a fractional CTO and founder of Pulsar Ventures, Sree has operated across engineering leadership, product strategy, consulting, sales, and growth. That cross-functional experience allows him to both architect AI-native solutions and translate them for founders, operators, and mixed technical audiences.

## Core Talk Tracks

Sree's talks center on three structural shifts:

### Leveraging AI Across the Maturity Spectrum

Moving across Assist, Automate, Augment, and Autonomy — helping teams evolve from AI as a support tool to AI embedded into workflows and operating systems.

### GROW, BUILD, and RUN Models

Designing organizational systems that integrate AI into strategy and execution — leveraging and operating through structured models that scale.

### AI-First Mindset + AI-Native Toolset

An *AI-first mindset* treats AI as an operating layer, not a feature. An *AI-native toolset* designs workflows that assume intelligence at every layer. Together, they mean rethinking tools, workflows, and decision models with intelligence as a foundational layer across the stack.
