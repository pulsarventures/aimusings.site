Workshops

Hands-on AI workshops designed for all skill levels. From beginner introductions to advanced platform-specific deep dives.

Workshop Agenda: Your AI Journey in One Day

AI Fundamentals & Mechanics

Discover how large language models work and the key principles behind effective AI prompting. Build a solid foundation for everything that follows.

AI Tools Deep Dive

Get hands-on with today's most powerful AI tools. Learn the unique strengths of each platform and when to use them for different tasks.

Build Your First Prototype

Apply your new knowledge to create a working prototype that solves a real problem in your business or career. Receive personalized guidance from our instructors.

Build Your First AI Agent

Take your skills to the next level by creating an automated AI agent that can handle complex tasks with minimal supervision.

Tools You'll Master

Our workshop gives you hands-on experience with today's most powerful AI tools, selected for their practical applications across different business needs.

Core

  • • ChatGPT and Gemini for versatile text generation, reasoning, and problem-solving
  • • Perplexity for research that cites sources
  • • Terminal/Command line access
  • • NotebookLM for research synthesis and organization
  • • Gamma for presentations and visual content

Prototyping & Development

  • • Lovable for rapid prototype development
  • • Replit for coding without development environment setup
  • • Cursor for AI-assisted coding and debugging
  • • Claude Code and Gemini CLI for command-line development workflows

Automation & Workflow

  • • MindStudio for building your first AI agents with guided templates
  • • n8n for advanced workflow automation with detailed control
  • • Langflow for advanced visual AI pipeline development
